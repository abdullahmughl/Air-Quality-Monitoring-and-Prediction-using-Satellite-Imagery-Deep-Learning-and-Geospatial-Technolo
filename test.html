Air Quality Awareness through AI


Air Quality Monitoring and Prediction using Satellite Spectral Data and state of the art Deep Learning Models

Proposed solution offers convenient and accurate air quality monitoring and prediction due to the following features:

FEATURE 1: Cost Effective.


FEATURE 2: Software based solution.


FEATURE 3: Better Spatial Coverage than on-ground monitoring stations.


FEATURE 4: Provide effective information communication, analysis and visualization.

How does it work?
A Deep Learning method based on Convolutional Neural Networks to predict air pollutants.
How do we collect data?
We use state of the art Sentinel-5 Precursor as our data source.
Visualize air quality data information in the form of graphs, tables and heatmaps.
Air quality data available for major pollutants of Nitrogen Dioxide, Sulphur Dioxide and Carbon Monoxide.
Forecasting models evaluated and updated every month.
